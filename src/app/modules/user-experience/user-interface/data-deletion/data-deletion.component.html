VirLetes APP ACCOUNT RETENTION POLICY AND TERMS OF USE



1.0 Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to define the length of time that VirLetes Sport Profile login accounts and associated services will be maintained after the account holder leaves the platform. It subsequently serves to maintain the security, operational cost, and efficiency of the VirLetes Sport Profile network.

2.0 Scope

This policy applies to all staff, and members who have login accounts on the VirLetes Sport Profile network.

3.0 Policy

VirLetes Sport Profile network login accounts and associated services (email, file storage, etc…) will be maintained for 30 days after the account holder leaves the network. After which, the login account, associated email and personal network storage space will be deleted.

3.1 Ownership and Responsibilities

3.1.1 Deletion Request
It is the member’s responsibility to submit an account deletion request at least 5 business days before the account holder’s last day of membership.

3.1.2 Account Holder
It is the member’s responsibility to ensure that they have copies of any desired data (email, contacts, etc…) before their last day of membership.

3.1.3 Network Resource Center
It is the Network Resource Center’s (NRC) responsibility to delete the account holder’s login account and data after the 30 days has expired.

3.2 Monitoring

The NRC periodically runs robotic scans to identify accounts that may no longer be in use. Members will be notified if such accounts are found in their name/email.

4.0 Enforcement

The VirLetes administrator or their delegate will be notified of violations of this policy, at which point they may attempt to work with the member directly. If these attempts are not successful, violation information may be passed along to the CTO of the VirLetes Sport Profile network for resolution.

5.0 Defitnitions

Term Definition
Login account A username and password given to the VirLetes Network member or staff that is used specifically for VirLetes Platform resources.
Account holder Any staff, or memeber of the VirLetes Network login account.
Email Account A User's email address associated with login account, typically login_account@domain.com.
File Storage A place where user data is stored. Typically data on the login account’s cloud: drive.
CTO Chief Technology Officer, manager for all VirLetes Network IT resources, who is the interface between VirLetes Network staff and IT resources.
Supervisor The individual responsible for supervision of the account holder.
System Administrator The title associated with the person responsible for core Business College IT resources, such as email, file server, and network access.
